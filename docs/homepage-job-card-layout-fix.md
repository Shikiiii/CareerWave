# Homepage job card layout fix

The homepage job cards were too cramped because the previous layout forced three cards per row while each card also contained a two-column internal company panel.

Changes made:
- Featured jobs now render in a maximum of two columns.
- Latest jobs now render in a maximum of two columns.
- Job cards only switch to their internal split layout on wider cards.
- Card padding and metadata spacing were slightly increased.
- The card visual style now better matches the company profile listing cards and avoids clipped text/icons.
